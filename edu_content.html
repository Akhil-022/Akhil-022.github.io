Right to Education

Click to learn more

Every child has the fundamental right to access quality education. This right ensures that children have the opportunity to learn, develop their skills, and reach their full potential. Education helps empower children and improves their future prospects..

Right to Health and Nutrition


Click to learn more

The right to health and nutrition ensures that children have access to adequate healthcare, nutritious food, and clean water. It safeguards their physical and mental well-being, promoting healthy growth and development..

Right to Protection from Abuse and Exploitation

Click to learn more

This right protects children from all forms of abuse, neglect, exploitation, and violence. It includes measures to prevent child labor, child trafficking, and any harm that could endanger a child's safety and well-being.
